Our Purpose…

As wanderlusts and global citizens, exchanging life stories with fellow globetrotters is typically the crescendo of our journey, and there's depth of experience in each and every one of us.

The COVID-19 pandemic and a series of worldwide events had turned our lives upside down.

Act! Action will delineate and define you.

zenyaga.com blog platform strives to collect people's account and life experiences living the zenyaga lifestyle - i.e. keeping a young and gifted spirit to overcome hardship, reminiscing and relating our great life experiences. Through which, uplift the spirits of the affected lives across the globe.

4 Time Escaper

“I was an artist - a drummer and great at it to boot - certain life events had forced me to stop 'living' awhile back. To a point of stagnancy. I had used music to drown the noises in my head, of the sorrows I couldn't get past, damaging my life and relationships along the way. The pandemic had given me a real time to reflect through solitude, and one step at a time, my friends and family had started to accept me back into their lives.”

Love C

6 Time Escaper

“Sometimes, it is easier to speak to strangers about your life woes in hopes of getting to some form of epiphany in our lives. There are certain expectations, and masks that we had put on in front of our close connections - family & friends. Finally, we reach a point where we tell ourselves that enough is enough, and we start to break. In my travels, I have met people who come from less fortunate circumstances than I have, yet they had maintained an optimistic life attitude. Somehow, exchanging these experiences has helped me see a different perspective, and restored a sense of gratitude into my life. ”
