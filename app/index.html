Life is full of ups and downs. Those are memories that make us all human.

Here’s how it all began…

As wanderlusts and global citizens, we would urge ourselves to travel the world whether it be for leisure or self-discovery. Exchanging life stories with fellow globetrotters is typically the crescendo of my journey. Few things I have learned: people are chiefly good, circumstances force us to be otherwise, and there's depth of experience in each and every one of us.

The tradition was brought on by positive influences & negative experiences in my life. There is no question that the COVID-19 pandemic and a series of worldwide events had turned our lives upside down.

When we hit rock bottom, the only way is up. Being human, we make mistakes (often due to indecision) and it's through honest introspection do we grow. This blog platform attempts to collect people's account and life experiences living the 'zenyaga' lifestyle, keeping a young and gifted spirit to overcome hardship, reminiscing and relating our great life experiences to uplift the spirits of people across the globe, as I have found it relevant in today's climate.

Our Features

Lifestyle Integration Products

Besides our content, zenyaga.com application offers thoughtful, quality, multipurpose products - practical elegance in looking long-term, and using products that represent, and simplify our lives - leaving time and space for the things that truly matter to us. More than ever, we need to keep ourselves healthy, in mind, body and spirit - human connections to overcome the tumultuous times that is, now.

Global Guides

Formal education teaches us to be contributing members of society, but life experiences are an entirely personal endeavour. Life experiences are color-blind, and cross borders. In relating inspiring stories by real life people, we hope to restore some semblance of hope in the lives of those who feel that they're lost and in need of replenishment in the form of connected and mindful sharing of life experiences that offer nuggets of wisdom to everyone no matter where they are in life.
